Paddock Club

What is Paddock Club?

The Formula 1 Paddock Club is a VIP hospitality package that offers access to exclusive amenities and services at Formula 1 Grand Prix events.

As a member, you will have the best views of the track from luxurious facilities, enjoy gourmet meals and open Champagne bars, and have the opportunity to get an up-close look at the sport of Formula 1.

In addition to the high-quality service and cuisine, members of the Paddock Club also have access to exclusive experiences such as pit lane walks and access to the Support Race Paddock.

The package includes a privileged viewing position, an official program, a pair of earplugs, entertainment, and more. All of these amenities are included with the purchase of a Paddock Club ticket, which also grants access to the circuit and privileged parking.
